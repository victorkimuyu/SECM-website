Our Story

A Legacy Built on Trust and Expertise

Established in 2003, SEC & M Valuers has grown into a premier Kenyan-owned property consulting firm. With over 20 years of active presence in the real estate industry, we have established ourselves as the benchmark for professional asset valuation and management.

Headquartered in Nairobi with strategic regional offices in Mombasa and Nakuru, we serve a diverse portfolio of clients, from major financial institutions and public sector entities to international NGOs and private investors.

Our team of highly qualified professionals is regulated by the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (RICS) and the Institution of Surveyors of Kenya (ISK), ensuring every engagement adheres to the highest global standards.

Our growing portfolio of clients includes major financial institutions, public sector entities, international NGOs, and private investors.
