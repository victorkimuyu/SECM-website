Residential Properties

From individual dwellings to expansive housing developments, we provide precision-driven valuations for residential assets. Our expertise covers mortgage assessments, insurance valuations, and pre-purchase advisory across Kenya's dynamic urban and rural landscapes.

  • Mansions, Maisonettes & Luxury residences
  • Apartment Blocks & High-rise Developments
  • Agricultural Farmhouses & Estates
  • Gated Housing Communities

Commercial Properties

Maximizing ROI requires a deep understanding of commercial market dynamics. We provide comprehensive valuations for commercial assets, utilizing income-capitalization models and comparative analysis to support investment, leasing, and portfolio management.

  • Grade-A Office Buildings
  • Regional Shopping Malls & Retail Centers
  • Industrial Warehouses & Logistics Hubs
  • Multi-use Integrated Complexes

Machinery & Equipment

Valuing technical assets requires engineering precision. Our specialized team assesses industrial installations and specialized equipment, focusing on depreciation cycles, salvage value, and operational life-cycles for financial reporting, insurance, and disposal.

  • Heavy Industrial Manufacturing Plants
  • Renewable Power Generation (Solar, Wind, Geothermal)
  • Specialized Medical & Laboratory Equipment
  • Agricultural & Construction Fleet Valuation

Special Assets

Unique assets require specialized methodologies. We specialize in the valuation of 'going-concern' entities and conservation-sensitive properties, ensuring that both business potential and environmental value are accurately represented.

  • Luxury Hotels, Resorts & Boutique Lodges
  • Private Wildlife Conservancies & Game Reserves
  • Specialized Recreational & Sport Facilities

Infrastructure Valuation

Standard valuation techniques often fall short for large-scale public and private infrastructure. We provide expert advisory for major utility and transport assets, supporting public-private partnerships (PPP) and national development frameworks.

  • National Highway Networks & Bridge Projects
  • Public Utilities (Water Treatment & Power Distribution)
  • Telecommunications & Connectivity Assets
