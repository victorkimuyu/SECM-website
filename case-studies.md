## Notable projects undertaken

The company boasts of over 20 years (combined) professional experience and has successfuly undertaken major valuations including but not limited to

### Pyrethrum Board of Kenya (PBK)
[image][image](./static/images/case-studies/pyrethrum-board.jpg)
Valuation of all PBK moveable and immovable assets within the country for Inventory, insurance and accounting purposes both in (Mar-June 2009 and April-May 2012): The subjects of valuation were plant and machinery including all motor vehicles, generators and others; Lands and buildings; Furniture and equipment; Fittings and fixtures and other office equipment.

### Narok County Council(Currently Narok County)
[image](./static/images/case-studies/narok-county.jpg)
Valuation of all moveable and immovable assets for Inventory and accounting purposes (July-Dec. 2008). The subjects of Valuation were plant and machinery including all motor vehicles, generators, airstrips, lands and buildings including the following: Maasai Mara Game Reserve (869.00 Sq Km), Maasai Mau forest (part of Mau Complex), Enoosupukia forest, Nemengio forest, Ilpoltan Tea Estate, Keekorok Lodge- Maasai Mara, Mara Serena Lodge, Mara Sarova lodge, Mara Simba lodge etc. Furniture and equipment, Fittings and fixtures and other office equipment.
